# LGIP-0008: Living God Economic Architecture

**Status:** Draft

**Category:** Tokenomics

**Author:** Living God Ecosystem

---

## Summary

This proposal establishes the guiding economic architecture of Living God Coin (LGC).

The objective is to create a sustainable economic system that supports entrepreneurship, ecosystem growth, and long-term institutional development.

---

## Guiding Principles

The Living God Economy is founded on five principles:

- Utility before speculation
- Long-term sustainability
- Responsible stewardship
- Community participation
- Entrepreneurial empowerment

---

## Fixed Supply

Maximum Supply:

15,000,000 LGC

No additional tokens shall be minted beyond the maximum supply unless modified through future governance.

## Endowment Philosophy

Rather than relying on automatic token burning, Living God Ecosystem establishes the Living God Endowment.

The Endowment is intended to preserve and responsibly manage strategic reserves that can support future ecosystem development, education, charitable initiatives, entrepreneurship, and long-term institutional sustainability.
