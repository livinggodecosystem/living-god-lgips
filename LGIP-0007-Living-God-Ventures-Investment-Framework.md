# LGIP-0007: Living God Ventures Investment Framework

**Status:** Accepted

**Category:** Venture Capital

**Author:** Living God Ecosystem

---

## Summary

This proposal establishes the official investment framework for Living God Ventures.

Living God Ventures exists to identify, support, and invest in founders building businesses that align with the mission of creating sustainable economic opportunity across Africa.

The framework balances ecosystem development with openness to exceptional founders throughout Africa.

---

## Investment Philosophy

Living God Ventures believes that long-term value is created by investing in founders of character, ideas of substance, and businesses that create lasting value.

Investment decisions shall consider both commercial sustainability and meaningful societal impact.

---

## Investment Tiers

### Tier I — Ecosystem Ventures

Highest priority shall be given to startups that are developed within the Living God Ecosystem.

These founders demonstrate commitment through participation in ecosystem programs, governance, education, and community engagement.

---

### Tier II — Mission-Aligned African Ventures

Living God Ventures may invest in outstanding African startups operating outside the Living God Ecosystem where they demonstrate strong alignment with the ecosystem's mission, values, and investment standards.

Innovation should never be limited by organizational boundaries.

---

### Tier III — Strategic Global Ventures

As the ecosystem matures, Living God Ventures may selectively invest in global companies whose technologies, partnerships, or expertise strengthen the Living God Ecosystem and create opportunities for African entrepreneurs.

---

## Investment Principle

> We invest in founders of character, ideas of substance, and businesses that create lasting value.

---

## Long-Term Objective

Living God Ventures seeks to become one of Africa's most trusted venture institutions by combining responsible investment, transparent governance, and long-term partnership with entrepreneurs.
